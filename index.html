About Event

Event description

The Internet of Things (IoT) and Artificial Intelligence (AI) profoundly change our professional and private lives. The IoT enables managing physical environments with software. Artificial Intelligence (AI) or more precisely Machine Learning enables new ways of processing data and identifying or predicting their development. The international winter school on "IoT meets AI" gathers lecturers from academia and industry to teach on the overlaps of the Internet of Things (IoT) and Artificial Intelligence (AI), discuss current challenges and mutually develop new ideas. Tutorials and workshops will be on topics such as IoT device integration, adaptation and interoperability, semantic technologies, IoT orchestration, data analytics and machine learning.Also The target audience of this winter school are students, researchers, developers and practitioners who are interested in acquiring knowledge and experience on IoT and the application of AI in this context. and cybersecurity and IoT.
